# Dự án: Mạng xã hội - LinkVerse

**Mô tả**: LinkVerse là một mạng xã hội hiện đại với các tính năng tương tác dễ sử dụng, giúp người dùng kết nối, chia
sẻ bài viết, hình ảnh, video, giao tiếp, và tham gia vào nhiều hoạt động trực tuyến.
Hệ thống được thiết kế như một nền tảng thông minh, ứng dụng trí tuệ nhân tạo (AI) và Machine Learning (ML) để cá nhân
hóa trải nghiệm, đề xuất nội dung phù hợp với sở thích, hành vi, và lịch sử tương tác của từng người dùng, giúp họ khám
phá và tương tác hiệu quả hơn. Ngoài ra, LinkVerse tích hợp AI để hỗ trợ người dùng, nâng cao trải nghiệm và tính tiện
lợi.

# Project: LinkVerse Social Network

**Description**:  
LinkVerse is a modern social network featuring user-friendly interactive functionalities that enable users to connect,
share posts, images, and videos, communicate, and engage in various online activities. The system is designed as an
intelligent platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to personalize experiences
and recommend content based on each user's interests, behaviors, and interaction history, helping them discover and
interact more effectively. Additionally, LinkVerse integrates AI to assist users and enhance convenience.

- **Login**: Users can log in via email or account credentials.
- **Registration**: Users can register using an email address and phone number.
- **Password Recovery**: Functionality to recover a forgotten password.

### 2. **Personal Profile – Manage Personal Page**

- **Edit Information**: Users can edit their personal details.
- **Activity Status**: Users can update their current status or activity.

### 3. **News Feed & Stories**

- **Display Posts**: The news feed shows posts and stories from friends and followed users.
- **Real-Time Updates**: The feed is updated automatically in real time.

### 4. **Post & Story Interactions (Comment, Like, Share)**

- **Interaction Options**: Users can like, comment on, and share posts.

### 5. **Create Posts – Manage Posts and Stories**

- **Create Posts**: Users can easily create and share posts.
- **Privacy Settings**: Users can adjust the privacy settings for each post.
- **Tagging and Location**: Ability to add tags and location information to posts.

### 6. **Friends & Following**

- **Add Friends**: Users can send and accept friend requests.
- **Follow**: Users can follow those they are interested in.
- **Manage Friends List**: Users can view, add, or remove friends and followers.

### 7. **Chat**

- **Messaging**: Users can send and receive messages to chat with friends.

### 8. **Notifications**

- **System Notifications**: Receive alerts from the system regarding new activities.

### 9. **Search**

- **User Search**: Find users by name or other identifying information.

- **Update Account Information**: Users can change their email, phone number, username, etc.
- **Login Activity Check**: View login history and sign out from unknown devices.
- **Activity History**: Access the history of interactions, comments, and posts.
- **Account Suspension**: Temporarily disable the account while retaining data.
- **Permanent Deletion**: Permanently remove all account information with no recovery option.

### 11. **Password Management and Recovery**

- **Change Password**: Change the password via the settings page.
- **Account Recovery**: Recover the account using email or phone number if the password is forgotten.

### 12. **Post Translation**

- **Automatic Language Detection**: AI detects if a post is written in a language different from the user's default

- **Automatic Translation**: Upon request, AI translates the post into the user's default language.
- **Customization Options**: Users can enable or disable automatic translation based on personal preference.

### 13. **Group Management**

- **Create Groups**: Users can create groups with settings for name, description, cover image, and theme. Privacy

- **Manage and Approve Members**: As group admins, users can add or approve new members, assign roles, moderate content,

### 14. **Page Management**

- **Create Pages**: Users can create pages by selecting the page type (e.g., business, brand, organization, media

- **Manage Content and Engagement**: Page owners can post content including text, images, videos, and events.
  Administrators can respond to comments and messages, as well as edit, delete, or hide posts when needed.

### 15. **VNP Payment Integration**

- **Seamless Transactions**: Users can complete transactions (e.g., for products or services) directly within LinkVerse
